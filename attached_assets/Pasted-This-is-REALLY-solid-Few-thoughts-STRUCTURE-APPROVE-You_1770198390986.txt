FORMATION STAGES - CRITICAL ADDITION:
Add "Failed" as a terminal stage for every pattern:
Cup Complete → Handle Forming → **Failed** (handle too deep, breaks cup)
T3 → T4 → **Failed** (contraction breaks down instead of contracting)
Why: AI needs to learn failure patterns as much as success patterns. This is how it learns "what not to take."

DATABASE TWEAK:
Add a 5th table: Rating Weights
Problem: Not all criteria matter equally for all timeframes.
Example:

Multi-year cup: Fundamentals matter MORE, intraday volume matters LESS
Intraday ORB: Relative volume matters MORE, multi-month RS line matters LESS

Solution:
Rating Weights Table:
- setup_variant_id
- criteria_id
- weight (0.0-1.0)
This lets you say:

"For Multi-Year Cup: fundamentals = 0.3, volume = 0.15"
"For 5-min ORB: relative volume = 0.35, fundamentals = 0.05"

AI learns context-dependent importance.
